-CossiePlay is a family-run business in Hulkstown since 2012.

-Cosplayers can order any kind of costume. Example Spiderman or elf costumes and advanced features like armour, animatronics, realistic weaponry and working force fields.

-Depending on the difficulty, costumes take between 1 and 50 weeks and cost between $10 and $10,000.

-We buy and sell second-hand costumes (Price list on "Our Costumes" section).

-On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall.
